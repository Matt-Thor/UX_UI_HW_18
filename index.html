My Work

Non-Profit Case Study

Non profit organizations often function for the benefit of others, however due to their non profit focus they may not have the means to finance the most current web technology.

We discovered an opportunity to provide a redesign concept for a local non profit.

We were able to create a modern design concept that prioritized access to information that our research uncovered as being most important.

Department of Education Website Case Study

Government agency websites often lack strong branding and design principles.Additionally they are usually built on old technology and cannot easily be updated.

By redesigning the Department of Education website with modern principalities and by creating a strong brand identity we were able to build a responsive design that users were able to navigate more intuitively and complete tasks in fewer steps.

Goal Assistant Case Study

Many people struggle with making and keeping their long and short term goals.

The Goal Assistant App is a solution that allows users to drop a loosely imagined idea in their head onto their mobile device to help them track their goals and keeps them engaged in completing long and short term goals.
